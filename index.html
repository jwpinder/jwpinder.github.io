Ride the Rapids

We believe that as you experience the outdoors with our experience Dry Oar guides, that you will add thrill and adventure into your life. Our trained guides will help you safely navegate the rapids of many of the famous rivers nearby. We offer rafting trips with rafts that can hold up to twenty people, so you can bring all of your friends along.

Sit Back and Relax

We believe that there is nothing like a river trip for relaxing. That is why we subscribe to the dry oar philosophy of boating. Keeping your oars dry for us means taking time to look around and notice the beauty that surrounds the rivers we love.

Plan Your Next Adventure

Dont miss out on our next adventure, we look forward to having you along and creating your next unforgetable trip. We take pride in having the most experienced and knowledgeable river trip guides anywhere. Our call center is open week days, so you can call and talk with one of our experienced guides, so that you can be well informed about your next adventure.
